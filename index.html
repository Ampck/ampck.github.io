Deep Pruning & Generation Logic

Parameters like Bias and Max Degree control graph birth, while Pruning Depth recursively cleans dead ends. The engine accounts for pruning loss by over-seeding the initial generation to ensure your Min Complexity is met.

Tactical Lane Disjointness

Entry lanes are calculated using node-disjoint pathfinding. The engine finds the shortest route, identifies the Entry Chokepoint (node before site), and recursively removes it to find alternative routes. Paths hugging the other site are penalized to ensure true strategic separation.

Strict Balance Guards

The engine validates every site individually. A map is only "Balanced" if Defenders reach ALL sites within the 15-35% headstart range. Rotation Tension scales with map size, representing the ratio of Site-to-Site time vs. Average Rush time.
